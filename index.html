Hospital Bag Essentials & Prep

Yay! Your bundle of joy is arriving soon! Packing your hospital bag can be fun. Here’s a lively teaser:

  • Comfy outfits for mom – soft robes, slippers, cozy socks!
  • Toiletries & pampering items – face wipes, lip balm, mini lotion
  • Important docs – ID, insurance, birth plan
  • Baby essentials – onesies, swaddles, diapers, cute hats!
  • Optional fun extras – camera, snacks, charger, playlist for labor
  • Tip: Adult diapers are medically okay for temporary postpartum incontinence – your body will bounce back soon!

Feeding Tips

Breastfeeding can be tricky, but we’re here to cheer you on! Teaser tips:

  • Softening engorged breasts before feeding helps a comfy latch
  • Warm compresses & gentle stimulation improve milk flow
  • Deep latch techniques make feeding smoother
  • Hydration & healthy diet help boost milk naturally

Sleep Tips
